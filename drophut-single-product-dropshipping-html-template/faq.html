Got questions about our apparel sizing guidelines, custom shipments, or material configurations? Explore our comprehensively detailed guide below to learn how we prioritize comfort, fabric longevity, and reliable door-to-door delivery operations across our capsule drops.

Every piece in our catalog features specific measurements shown within its dedicated sizing table. For oversized capsules like the Urban Bomber Jacket, we recommend buying your regular size for the intended loose fit profile, or sizing down if you prefer a more tailored daily silhouette configuration.

We source heavy-weight combed cotton fabric variations, durable technical outer membranes, and wind-resistant outer shell materials. These choices isolate your body from seasonal elements while offering dynamic stretch retention capabilities across high-stress points.

To preserve textile structure vibrance and avoid fiber shrinkage, turn clothing items inside out before machine washing them in cold water. We recommend air-drying or using low tumble settings, especially for combed cotton shirts or weather-resistant utility jackets.

Orders are packaged and dispatched from our local fulfillment facility within 24 to 48 hours. Standard courier routes take roughly 3 to 5 business days, and real-time live tracking data links are delivered automatically via email once packages register with logistic carriers.
